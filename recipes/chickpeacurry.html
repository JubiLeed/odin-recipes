Coconut Curry Chickpeas with Pumpkin and Lime

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons avocado oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, pressed or minced
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • Shake of cayenne pepper to taste
  • 1 15-ounce can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 15-ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 13.5-ounce can coconut cream
  • 2-3 teaspoons maple syrup
  • Sea salt
  • Juice of 1-2 limes
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • lime wedges for garnish

    • Description

    This quick creamy vegan coconut chickpea curry is a super easy and speedy recipe that’s big on flavor. With tomatoes, coconut cream, and the perfect blend of spices, it’s so creamy and filling! This chickpea curry is bound to be a healthy dinner staple for your family, whether you’ve been vegan for years or are just trying out Meatless Monday!

    Steps

    1. Heat a bit of oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Sauté your chopped onion until it’s softened and translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook, stir constantly, until fragrant, about 30-60 seconds.
    2. Add the spices and cook, stirring regularly, until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
    3. Stir in the drained chickpeas and can of tomatoes. Simmer about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until chickpeas are softened and tomatoes are reduced slightly.
    4. Stir in the coconut cream, maple syrup, and plenty of salt. Whisk until the coconut cream is smooth and incorporated. Simmer 5 minutes.
    5. Finally, stir in the fresh lime juice and chopped cilantro. Serve over basmati or cauliflower rice and garnish with more chopped cilantro and lime slices.
