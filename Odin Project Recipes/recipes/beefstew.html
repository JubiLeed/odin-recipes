Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chuck roast cut into 2" chunks
  • 1 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1/2 yellow onion chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 carrots cut into 2 inch chuncks
  • 2 Yuon potatoes chopped
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

    • Description

    This quick creamy vegan coconut chickpea curry is a super easy and speedy recipe that’s big on flavor. With tomatoes, coconut cream, and the perfect blend of spices, it’s so creamy and filling! This chickpea curry is bound to be a healthy dinner staple for your family, whether you’ve been vegan for years or are just trying out Meatless Monday!

    Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees and season the beef chunks with salt and pepper before dredging in flour.
    2. Add the butter and canola oil to a large dutch oven on medium heat and brown the beef on all sides in batches (3-4 minutes per batch).
    3. Remove the last batch of the beef and add in the onions, garlic and carrots and cook for 2-3 minutes until slightly caramelized before adding the potatoes in along with the broth, tomato paste, bay leaf, thyme and Worcestershire sauce and stirring to combine.
    4. Stir in the coconut cream, maple syrup, and plenty of salt. Whisk until the coconut cream is smooth and incorporated. Simmer 5 minutes.
    5. Add the beef back into the pot, cover and cook in the oven for 3 hours, removing the bay leaf before serving.
